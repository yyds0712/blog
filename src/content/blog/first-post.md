Welcome to **AI with Me**.

This site has been a long time in the making, and the name took even longer to settle. Now that it's finally live, I want to share the story behind it.

## Why AI with Me?

The domain "aiwithme" has two meanings.

**Layer 1: AI & Me.** My college years have coincided with the fastest period of AI development. Using ChatGPT for lab reports, AI tools for group projects, Cursor for coding, Claude for writing, Gamma for presentations... AI has become part of my daily life. As someone going to college in the AI era, these experiences are worth documenting.

**Layer 2: AI With Me.** It's a play on words. AI is not just with me — I hope you'll be with me too. Every article has a comment section where you can join the conversation.

## What You'll Find Here

Campus life, AI tools reviews, programming notes, productivity tips. Nothing too serious — more like a senior student's diary.

## Why Not Social Media?

Being pushed around by algorithms doesn't feel good. Writing on my own little corner of the internet is more freeing — I write what I want.
